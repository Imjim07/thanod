How Thanod works

Three layers of yield generation running continuously. Set your preferences once — Thanod handles everything else.

01

Connect Your Account

Link your crypto exchange via API key or connect your Web3 wallet. Thanod is non-custodial — your assets remain on your exchange at all times.

02

Choose Your Tier

Select Starter, Growth, or Premium based on your investment level and risk appetite. Each tier unlocks different strategies and expected returns.

03

Thanod Goes to Work

Staking rewards compound automatically. The trading bot executes strategies around the clock. The Portfolio Brain monitors and rebalances in real time.

04

Track Everything

Your dashboard shows total balance across all layers, live APR projections, bot performance, real estate allocation, and full transaction history.

Our Story

Built on the belief
that idle crypto
is wasted potential.

The Vision

Most people who hold crypto do nothing with it. It sits in wallets, on exchanges, slowly losing ground to inflation. Thanod was built to change that.

We believe financial automation — once available only to institutions — should be accessible to anyone who holds crypto, regardless of how much or how little they know about markets.

Thanod brings staking, algorithmic trading, and intelligent rebalancing together in one non-custodial, risk-managed platform.

Where We Stand

Thanod is currently in its pre-launch phase. We are building, testing, and preparing for a full platform release. Our waitlist is open and early members receive priority access when the platform goes live.

We are committed to transparency above everything else. No promised returns. No hidden fees. Full verifiability of staking rewards.

Three investment tiers — Starter, Growth, and Premium — give every user a clear, structured path into automated crypto investment.

Core Values
Transparency

Real numbers. No performance theatre. Users see exactly what is happening with their assets at all times.

Non-Custody

Your keys, your crypto. Thanod operates via API keys and smart contracts. We are architects of automation — not custodians of your wealth.

Risk First

We design for protection before profit. Safety architecture built into the core — drawdown caps, stop-loss, slashing insurance.
