How withdrawals work
1
Enter the amount and the wallet address you want your funds sent to. This can be any wallet you own — exchange address, hardware wallet, or personal wallet.
2
Submit your request. The Thanod team will review it manually and process it within 3 – 30 minutes. Timing may vary depending on your broker and network conditions.
3
Once processed, the transaction will appear in your Transaction History. You'll receive the funds at your specified address.
4
Withdrawals are irreversible. Always verify the destination address before submitting.
Important: Withdrawal requests are processed manually by the Thanod team. Processing time is typically 3 – 30 minutes but may take longer depending on network congestion and your broker. Ensure the destination address is correct — Thanod cannot recover funds sent to a wrong address.
